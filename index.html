The Method

The M.I.R.R.O.R. Framework.

Six dimensions of self-inquiry. Woven into every day of your 90.

M
Mindfulness

Present before the noise begins. Every session starts here.

I
Inquiry

The questions that make you stop. Those are the only ones that count.

R
Reflection

Looking back without flinching. Patterns only emerge when you look.

R
Responsibility

Own your choices. No exceptions, no audience, no excuses.

O
Openness

Honesty when honesty is the harder path. The practice only works if you do.

R
Renewal

End each day with intention. Not perfection. Direction.

The 90 Days

Five phases.
One transformation.

Ninety days isn't arbitrary. It's how long real change actually takes.

I

Days 1 – 18

Awakening

Before anything can change, you have to see where you actually are. Clearly, without softening it. Day one is an arrival, not a transformation.

II

Days 19 – 36

Excavation

You start digging. The prompts go deeper, into the stories you inherited, the defenses you built, the versions of yourself you perform without realising it.

III

Days 37 – 54

The Turn

The hardest phase. Everything shifts here, not because circumstances change, but because you choose a different lens. Old stories get examined.

IV

Days 55 – 72

Pattern Work

You stop reacting and start watching. Patterns you couldn't see day to day become visible. Your journal and app insights show you the shape of your own mind.

V

Days 73 – 90

Rising

The practice becomes the life. Day 90 is not an ending. It's the first day you don't need to be told who you are.
