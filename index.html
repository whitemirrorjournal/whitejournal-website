The Method

The M.I.R.R.O.R. Framework.

Six dimensions of self-inquiry. Woven into every day of your 90.

M
Mindfulness

Present before the noise begins. Every session starts here.

I
Inquiry

The questions that make you stop. Those are the only ones that count.

R
Reflection

Looking back without flinching. Patterns only emerge when you look.

R
Responsibility

Own your choices. No exceptions, no audience, no excuses.

O
Openness

Honesty when honesty is the harder path. The practice only works if you do.

R
Renewal

End each day with intention. Not perfection — direction.

The 90 Days

Three phases.
One transformation.

Ninety days isn't arbitrary. It's how long real change actually takes.

I

Days 1 – 30

The Reckoning

You look at what you've been avoiding. Not with judgment — with honesty. The story you've been telling yourself meets the truth.

II

Days 31 – 60

The Work

Awareness without action is observation. This is where the patterns get named, and the repeating finally stops.

III

Days 61 – 90

The Integration

Not a conclusion — a beginning. You define who you're becoming and build the practice you carry forward.
